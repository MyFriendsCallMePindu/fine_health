About Fine Health Diagnostic & Medical Center

Promoting quality of life through accessible and compassionate healthcare

Fine Health Diagnostic & Medical Center was established to meet the growing healthcare needs of the community. We believe quality healthcare should be accessible, affordable, and delivered with compassion.

Located in George Compound, Lusaka, our clinic serves as a trusted healthcare partner for families, mothers, children, and individuals seeking professional medical attention.

We focus on prevention, early diagnosis, and effective treatment to improve quality of life in our community.

Our Core Purpose

Mission Statement

To provide the people of Lusaka and surrounding areas equitable access to primary health care services.

Vision Statement

To bridge the gap between disease burden and quality affordable healthcare services.
